Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about our services.

How do I register on the portal?
Click on the "Register" button and fill in your personal information, then upload the required documents for verification.
What documents do I need?
You need your CV, National ID, Passport, Academic Certificates, Professional Certificates, and a passport-size photo.
How long does verification take?
Verification typically takes 2-3 business days. You will be notified via email once your account is verified.
What payment methods are accepted?
We accept M-Pesa, eCitizen, Bank Transfer, Visa, and Mastercard for government employment service fees.
