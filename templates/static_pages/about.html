What We Do

Our comprehensive approach to connecting citizens with verified employment opportunities.

Job Verification

All job listings are thoroughly verified by government officials and accredited partners to ensure authenticity and legitimacy.

Employer Accreditation

Employers and recruitment agencies undergo a rigorous accreditation process before they can list opportunities on the portal.

Secure Payments

Government employment service fees are processed through secure, government-approved payment channels with full transparency.

Application Tracking

Citizens can track their application progress in real-time through their personalized dashboard with status updates.

Our Journey

Key milestones in our mission to connect citizens with verified employment opportunities.

2026 Full Platform Launch

Official launch of the Government Jobs Portal with comprehensive features for citizens, employers, and recruitment agencies.

2025 International Expansion

Partnerships established with multiple international employers and foreign embassies for overseas job opportunities.

2024 Pilot Program

Successful pilot program with selected employers and recruitment agencies demonstrating the platform's effectiveness.

2023 Platform Development

Development of the Government Jobs Portal by the Ministry of Labour in collaboration with development partners.
